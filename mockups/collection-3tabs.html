📝 Learnings · jadi reference buat collection berikutnya

What worked:
  • KOL Hindia + Voldemort generated 32 attributable orders on launch week — book early next time
  • Pop-up event di Senayan AOV-nya 17% lebih tinggi dari online — consider repeat formula
  • Premium SKU (LS Brown, Smock Jacket) drove ASP up — fans willing to spend
What didn't:
  • TikTok Ads ROI 0.9× — algorithm gak nyangkut untuk IDGITAF audience
  • Bundle Sedia Aku terlalu mahal di Rp 250k — convert rendah; consider unbundle next time
  • Size XXXL & S over-produced (15% sell-through avg) — tighten size distribution
For next collection:
  • Target Rp 130-150M reasonable based on +52% growth trajectory
  • Focus production: 50% M/L, 30% XL, 10% XXL, 5% S/XXXL each
  • Allocate 30% marketing budget ke KOL (proven), 40% ke Meta Ads, 25% offline event, 5% PR
